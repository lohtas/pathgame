Frequency Distribution Graph Puzzle

Frequency distribution graphs shows how a set of quantitative values are spread across their entire range.

Potential encoding methods:

  • Bars (vertical only) to emphasize individual values Note: This kind of graph is called a histogram
  • Lines to emphasize the overall shape of the data Note: This kind of graph is called a frequency polygon.

Determine which type of data point is the most effective for this graph and drag and drop it into place.
