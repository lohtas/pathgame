Congratulations! You completed your Information Visualization skills training. Graph on and prosper!


Keep in mind, Stephen Few's step-by-step process is meant to serve as guidelines for creating more efficient graphs. Most data sets, especially those with more than two variables, will have more than one method or solution to its visualization technique. Visualizations are hardly ever right or wrong, but rather sit on a quality spectrum. Some graphs convey data better than others.
