Part-to-Whole Graph Puzzle

In part-to-whole graphs, quantitative values are displayed to reveal the portion that each value represents to some whole.

Potential encoding methods:

  • Bars (horizontal or vertical)
  • Use stacked bars only when you must display measures of the whole as well as the parts

Determine if bars or pie charts are more appropriate for the data. If bars, drag and drop each one into its correct place. (Hint: Focus on the length/quantity of the 'Available' [yellow] segment of each bar to determine the correct place.) If pie charts, drag all of them into the graph matrix.
