Time-Series Graph Puzzle

In a time series graph, quantitative values are expressed as a series of measures taken across equal intervals of time. This type of graph reveals trends and patterns.


Potential encoding methods:

  • Lines to emphasize the overall shape of the data
  • Bars to emphasize and support comparisons between individual values
  • Points connected by lines to slightly emphasize individual values while still highlighting the overall shape of the data


Determine which elements of the graph are most appropriate to use with each other and place in the correct locations. Not all elements will be used.
