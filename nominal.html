Nominal Comparison Graph Puzzle

In nominal comparison graphs, no particular relationship between the values exist. Nominal categories are simply compared.

Potential encoding methods:

  • Bars (horizontal or vertical)
  • Points (if the quantitative scale does not include zero)

Determine which set of bars are appropriate for this graph and drag and drop them into place.
