Ranking Graph Puzzle

In a ranking type of graph, Quantitative values are sequenced by size.


Potential encoding methods:

  • Bars (horizontal or vertical)
  • Points (if the quantitative scale does not include zero)

This puzzle asks that you follow Few's fourth step, "Determine where to display each variable". Determine an appropriate place to label each bar with its corresponding book title.
